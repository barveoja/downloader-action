Common Questions

Why would I want to edit my audio file tags?

Proper tagging helps organize your music library, ensures correct display in media players, and makes it easier to find songs by artist, album, or genre.

Is my music quality affected by editing tags?

No, we only modify the metadata (tags) in your files, leaving the audio quality completely unchanged. Your music will sound exactly the same.
